What is Unity Wave?

UnityWave is an innovative platform designed to connect individuals based on their skills, interests, and goals, forming cohesive and highly effective team. This is done by a great algorithm that selects individuals based on their skills and knowledge. Giving all to the community and getting a great team is key, to a successful project. This tends to make some great matchups and giving the user the best quality they can. The best thing is that is easy to use and not complex to use. The idea behind all this is to create a secure platform where you as a user can find a friend, a group partner or even a person that has the atributes that you don't have.
